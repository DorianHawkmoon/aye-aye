Descripción:

Text adventure game.

A simple character who wants to spend his vacations in the country house, but it is full of vermin. The goal is to kill them. You lose if you die.

Extra features:

Besides moving, take and drop objects (inside other objects allowed), following features are available

  • Combat system by turns with one or more enemies, can attack, escape (not always posible) or equip and eat to modify stats and life
  • Dead enemies drop a sack with objects (by now only the ferret drop objects)
  • stats of character, life/attack/defense
  • weapons and armors that can be equiped, modifying stats
  • food and potions to recover life, even in combat
  • differents types of doors, some of them needs objects to be opened
  • special doors/windows (example: a door can be opened by one side, but the other side needs an object)
  • some items are containers, can be opened or closed and store other objects
  • some items can be picked up
  • inventory of character and criatures

Comandos:

  • look [room|inventory]
  • go south|west|east|north|up|down
  • see [_item] [[in] inventory] es decir, see letter, see letter in inventory ó see letter inventory. Da algo más de información que look, pero ambos son muy similares. Dato, para leer la carta, usar see
  • open _item|_nameExit|_direction
  • take _item
  • drop _item [into|in] [_item] drop letter into mailbox ó drop letter mailbox
  • close _item|_nameExit|_direction
  • equip _item
  • unequip _item
  • stats
  • eat _item

during combat, available commands are:

  • escape
  • attack _nameEnemy
  • eat _nameItem
  • look
  • equip _nameItem
  • unequip _nameItem

Walkthrougth:

Erratum: during the game we need to eat something (eat _item), but is not especified when.

You start in the forrest with your boxmail
  • open boxmail (opcional)
  • see letter (opcional)
  • take knife
  • equip knife
  • go south
  • attack vole
Now we are in front of the house
  • open box
  • take key
  • open door
  • go south
We are in the kitchen, take food
  • take can
  • take cupcake
  • take bread
  • go west
Attack! fight against them and we are in the bedroom, where we take meat, a gun and an armor
  • attack ferret
  • open remains-ferret
  • take meat
  • open wardrobe
  • take hoe
  • take overalls
  • equip hoe
  • equip overalls
  • go east
Again in the kitchen, we go at one side of the house
  • go north
  • go west
  • attack snake
We are in a garden with a trapdoor, we open it and go down
  • open trapdoor
  • go down
  • attack rat
  • attack rat
  • attack giantspider (repeat)
  • take ladder
  • open up
  • go up
we go to the other side of the house
  • go east
  • go east
  • attack vole
  • attack vole
You win

To lose, fight without armor and without eating
